# About Glenn Bennett

Glenn Bennett (aka Bennett) performs a collection of folk-rock, soft rock with a sprinkling of pop, country-rock, and original material. From artists like the Eagles and Jackson Browne.

Not only does he play guitar and sing, but he uses a custom rhythm section to create a one-person band. Glenn's unique setup gives his sound a toe-tapping upbeat feel.

## Achievements

- **"I've Got to Find Out for Myself"** reached #22 on the American Idol Underground Folk Music Chart (May 2006)
- **Feel Good Track** in Folk Rock on GarageBand.com (February 2006)
