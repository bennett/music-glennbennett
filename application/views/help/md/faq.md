# Frequently Asked Questions

## Why can't I hear anything?

- Check your device volume (not just the in-app volume)
- Make sure your phone isn't in silent mode
- Try using headphones to test
- Check if another app is using audio

## How do I get the app on my home screen?

See the **Installing the App** help topic for step-by-step instructions for iPhone, iPad, and Android.

## Can I download songs for offline listening?

Songs you've played recently are automatically cached. While there's no explicit download button, the app will play cached songs if you lose internet connection.

## How do I find my favorites?

Your favorited songs (marked with a heart) are saved to your device. Look for the Favorites section in the app.

## Does this work with Bluetooth speakers?

Yes! Bennett Music works with any Bluetooth audio device, including speakers, headphones, and car systems.

## Is there a cost?

No, Bennett Music is completely free to use.

## Can I use this on my computer?

Yes! Open the site in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari on any computer. You can even install it as a desktop app from Chrome.

## How do I contact Glenn?

Visit [GlennBennett.com/contact](https://glennbennett.com/contact) to send a message, or check the [calendar](https://glennbennett.com/cal) for upcoming live performances.

## The app seems stuck or broken

Try these steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Check for updates (Menu → Check for Updates)
3. Clear your browser cache if using the web version
4. Reinstall the app from Safari
