# Features

## High-Quality Streaming

All songs are streamed in high-quality audio, delivered through a fast CDN for smooth playback.

## Background Playback

Music continues playing when you switch apps or lock your screen. Control playback from:
- Lock screen controls
- Control Center (iOS) / Notification shade (Android)
- Headphone/earphone buttons

## Offline Support

Recently played songs are cached on your device. If you lose internet connection, you can still listen to songs you've played before.

## Favorites

Mark songs as favorites with the heart icon. Your favorites are saved locally and persist between sessions.

## Shuffle & Repeat

- **Shuffle** - Randomize the play order
- **Repeat All** - Loop the entire album
- **Repeat One** - Loop the current song

## Smart Playback

A brief pause between songs gives each track its own moment, just like listening to a curated playlist.

## Album Artwork

Each song displays its cover art in the player and on your lock screen.
