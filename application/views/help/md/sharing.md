# Sharing Music

Share Glenn's music with friends and family!

## Share the App

From the menu, tap **Share This App** to send a link. On mobile, this opens your device's share sheet so you can text, email, or AirDrop the link.

## Share via QR Code

Tap **Show QR Code** in the menu to display a scannable code. Perfect for sharing in person - just let someone scan it with their phone camera.

## Share an Album

When viewing an album, tap the **Share Album** button to send a link directly to that album. When your friend opens it, they'll go straight to the album you shared.

## Share a Song

In the expanded player, tap the **Share** button to share the currently playing song. Your friend will open the app right to that track.

## What Friends See

When someone opens a shared link:
- They see the album or song you shared
- They can start playing immediately
- They can explore other music too
- No account or login required
