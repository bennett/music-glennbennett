# Getting Started

Welcome to Bennett Music! Here's how to enjoy the music.

## Browsing Music

Tap an **album cover** to see all its songs. The album will expand to show the track list.

Tap any **song** to start playing. The player bar appears at the bottom.

## Player Controls

The mini player at the bottom shows what's playing. Tap it to expand for more controls:

- **Play/Pause** - Start or stop playback
- **Next/Previous** - Skip between tracks
- **Progress bar** - Drag to seek within a song
- **Volume** - Adjust playback volume

## Favorites

Tap the **heart icon** on any song to save it as a favorite. Your favorites are saved to your device so you can quickly find the songs you love.

## Background Playback

Music keeps playing when you:
- Switch to other apps
- Lock your screen
- Use your phone normally

Control playback from your lock screen or control center.
