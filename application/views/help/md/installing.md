# Installing the App

For the best experience, install Bennett Music as an app on your device. It's free and takes just a few seconds.

## iPhone & iPad

1. Open this site in **Safari** (not Chrome or other browsers)
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** in the top right

The app icon will appear on your home screen!

## Android

1. Open this site in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **menu** (three dots in the top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**
4. Tap **Add**

## Benefits of Installing

- **Full screen** - No browser address bar
- **App icon** - Easy access from your home screen
- **Better performance** - Runs like a native app
- **Offline access** - Play cached songs without internet
- **CarPlay support** - Works with your car's system

## Desktop

On Mac or Windows, you can also install from Chrome:
1. Click the install icon in the address bar, or
2. Click menu → "Install Bennett Music"
