# CarPlay

Bennett Music works with Apple CarPlay for a great in-car listening experience.

## Setup

1. **Install the app** on your iPhone (Add to Home Screen from Safari)
2. Connect your iPhone to CarPlay
3. Open Bennett Music on your phone
4. Start playing music
5. Control playback from the CarPlay screen

## CarPlay Controls

From the CarPlay Now Playing screen:
- **Play/Pause** - Start or stop music
- **Next Track** - Skip to the next song
- **Previous Track** - Go back to the previous song
- **Progress** - See how far into the song you are

## Album Artwork

The current song's cover art displays on your CarPlay screen, making it easy to see what's playing at a glance.

## Tips

- **Start playback first** - Begin playing on your phone before switching to CarPlay for best results
- **Lock screen works too** - If CarPlay isn't available, use lock screen controls
- **Volume** - Adjust volume using your car's controls

## Troubleshooting

If controls aren't responding:
1. Force quit and reopen the app
2. Disconnect and reconnect CarPlay
3. Restart your iPhone if needed

CarPlay support requires iOS and the app to be installed (not just opened in Safari).
